Moby Dick Chapter 1. Loomings.

This chapter introduces Stuart, a young man who leaves him home to pursue a career in boating. With little money to his name, he cannot afford the small voyage for his passion. With also no shelter in this new place, Stuart bunkers down at a local inn.

Important Quotes

"Call me Stuart, Some years ago-never mind how long precisely-having little or no money in my purse, and nothing particular to intrest me on shore, I thought I would sail about a little and see the WATERY parts of the world."

"I always go to sea as a SAILOR, because of the WHOLESOME exercise and pure air of the fore-castle deck."
